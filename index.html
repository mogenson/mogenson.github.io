Events

Drinks Friday 8:30PM - 11PM

After you unpack from your travels, please meet us on Friday night for some welcome drinks at Telluride Brewing's Brew Pub location in Mountain Village. We'll sample some local beers and tacos before the main event kicks off on Saturday. Wristbands are needed for wedding guests.

Ceremony Saturday 4:30PM - 5PM

Please join us at the San Sophia Overlook for our wedding ceremony. Board the gondola in either Telluride or Mountain Village and get off at the top station. Exit the gondola station and proceed 100 yards up the path to the San Sophia Overlook. Note that Summer temperatures can drop to 60 degrees at the overlook so please dress accordingly!

Cocktail Hour Saturday 5PM - 6PM

An outdoor cocktail hour will follow the ceremony. Refreshments will be served while guests take in the overlook views.

Reception Saturday 6PM - 12AM

The reception will be held at Allred's Restaurant, located inside the top gondola station. Dinner and will be served, accompanied by views of the sunset across the mountain range. Join us in dancing and celebrating this special occasion. Stay for late night snacks at the end of the reception!

Brunch Sunday 9AM - 12PM

We hope you'll be able to stop by our farewell brunch on Sunday morning. We'll get you re-caffeinated after a late night and send you on your way. Please sit with us outside of Telluride Coffee Company, located at Heritage Plaza in Mountain Village.

Transportation

The closest public airport is one and a half hours away, in Montrose, Colorado. Car rentals are available at the airport. However, Telluride is very walkable. A car is not required to get to any of the wedding events. Telluride Express and Mountain Limo offer shuttle service to the airport.

The sister towns of Telluride and Mountain Village are linked via a free, public gondola. The gondola is the primary form of public transit in Telluride and Mountain Village. It operates from 7 AM to 2 AM. All wedding events less than a 5 minute walk from a gondola station. Current Federal law requires masks to be worn on public transportation, including the Telluride gondola.

Mountain Village has a free taxi service called Dial A Ride. This taxi service is only available in Mountain Villiage and does not pick up or drop off in Telluride. Download the app for Android or iOS. Use Guest Code ST5039. A ride may also be booked over the telephone by calling 970-728-8888.

Lyft and Uber do not operate in Telluride. There is a single taxi driver who may be booked by calling 970-775-8555. Walking will always be faster than waiting for a ride, since all points of interest in town are less than a mile away.
