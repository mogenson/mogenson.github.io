Getting here

The closest public airport is Montrose, Colorado, with service from American, United, and Delta airlines. Telluride is a 1 hour 30 minute drive from Montrose. Car rentals are available at the airport. However, since you don't need a car to get around town, we encourage guests to book a shuttle from the airport with either Telluride Express or Mountain Limo.

US News has listed Telluride as the #1 Best Small Town to visit so we hope it's worth your travel!

Where to stay

The sister towns of Telluride and Mountain Village are linked via a free, public gondola. Telluride offers walkable bars, restaurants, cafés, and shopping in a historic mining town. Mountain Village has 360 degree panoramic views, larger accommodations, and a free Dial-A-Ride taxi service.
